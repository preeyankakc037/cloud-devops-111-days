    Normal AWS: I go to the resturant              
    Outposts: Resturant opens a kitchen inside my house.

    Why to use it: 
    * We can keep data inside our company.
    * We will get very less latency. 

    Outposts = Hybrid Cloud    (Combination of cloud + your own data center)

* **AWS LocalZones**

    >**AWS Local Zones** are small AWS setups placed near big cities so that applications can run closer to users and respond faster. Instead of sending data all the way to a far AWS region, Local Zones allow services like servers and storage to be nearby, reducing delay and improving performance. They are fully managed by AWS, and are mainly used for applications that need very quick response times, such as gaming, video streaming, and real-time apps.


* **How to select and AWS Region**

    >The choice of an AWS Region implies the selection of the most optimal location where your application will be hosted, depending on the needs. We encouraged to select a Region that is compliant with the data laws in our country to keep your data within its jurisdiction, select one near your users to have our app run faster with less latency, ensure the services you require are available in that Region, and also compare prices since costs may vary in each Region.
